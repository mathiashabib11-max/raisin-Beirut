Meet Our Team

The passionate people behind Raisin' Beirut - all from Lebanese families who taught us the love of cooking!

Mathias Habib

Owner

Hey, I'm Mathias! I started Raisin' Beirut because I wanted to share the amazing flavors I grew up with. Coming from a Lebanese family, food was always at the center of everything - Sunday lunches at my grandmother's house, the smell of spices in the kitchen, watching my mom cook for hours. I learned that food isn't just about eating, it's about bringing people together. That's the vibe we bring to Raisin' Beirut!

Jasson Gebrael

Head Cook

What's up! I'm Jasson, the one making all that crispy chicken magic happen. My love for cooking started when I was like 10 years old, helping my dad in our family kitchen back in Lebanon. He taught me all the secrets - how to marinate meat just right, how to get that perfect crisp on fried chicken, how to balance spices. Every recipe we use here has a piece of my family's history in it. Cooking is my passion, and I'm hyped to share it with y'all!

MR Eddy

Waiter

Hi everyone! I'm eddy, and I'm usually the one greeting you with a smile when you walk in. Growing up Lebanese, hospitality is basically in my blood - my mom always taught me that guests are a blessing. I love meeting new people and making sure everyone feels welcome here. Plus, I get to recommend my favorite dishes (get the chicken strips, trust me). Come say hi next time you're here!
